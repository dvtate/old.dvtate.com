The forge

I made my first forge in the 7th grade. It was a flower-pot forge which ran on charcoal (which I made at home). I used it only once. I eventually switched to coal, and built a new forge (the one in the video). This worked great for me. I made jewellery which I sold to make some money. I am in the process of switching to propane, once I create my forge, I will start making jewellery again. Thanks for the support!
